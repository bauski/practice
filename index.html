The Practice

The One

The Tao is nameless and ever present
Ever eternal and ever changing
The primordial form and mother of the ten thousand things
What can be named is only a facet of it's true splendor
To walk the path is to be in practice
To walk the path is to be in play
To find your path is to find the Tao
To find your path is to explore the ten thousand things
When we align ourselves to the path we are aware of the Tao
